Questions people ask

Is this a dating app?

No. Zizo matches you on shared interests — padel partners, brunch crews, gym buddies, board-game nights. Group settings are the default.

How does cashback actually work?

Visit a partner venue, tap to check in, pay as normal. A percentage of your bill (set by the venue, usually 5–15%) lands in your in-app wallet within an hour. Spend it at any other partner venue. Visit with a match — it doubles.

Where in Qatar does it work?

Launching across Doha, Lusail, and the Pearl with our first cohort of partner venues. Adding Al Wakrah and Education City next.
