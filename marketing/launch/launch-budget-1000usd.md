# Zizo Circle — $1,000 Launch Budget (4-Week Plan, Qatar)

> Currency reference: $1,000 USD ≈ **QAR 3,640** (1 USD ≈ 3.64 QAR).
> Strategy: sign venues first → activate one anchor micro-influencer (TBD) → user push → optimize.
> Every spend either (a) buys a venue, (b) buys credibility, or (c) buys a verified user. No brand awareness for its own sake.

**Targets:**

- One strong creator > five weak ones at this budget level

| Item | Cost | Notes |

| Referral incentive — first 30 referrers get extra QAR 25 | $25 | "Invite a friend who joins → +QAR 25 to both wallets" |
| Email tooling (Resend or Loops, free → starter) | $20 | Spin up a 3-email welcome sequence |
| Buffer — paid spend overflow / unexpected | $5 | |
| **Week 4 total** | **$188** | |

**Targets:**
- WAU/MAU ratio > 0.45
- 25%+ of sign-ups complete one cashback redemption
- Referral loop contributes 10%+ of new sign-ups
- 12 venue partners now showing actual Zizo-attributed traffic

> Wallet seed math: $200 wallet seed budget = QAR 730 ≈ ~14 users at QAR 50 each. To hit "first 100," structure it as **first 14 to complete a real cashback visit** rather than first 100 to sign up — converts a marketing spend into a behavior-shaping spend. Cap is enforced; signups beyond 14 get a smaller QAR 20 credit funded by venue-side cashback economics, not Zizo's marketing budget.

## What's deliberately NOT in this budget

| Item | Why we skipped it |
|---|---|
| Google / Apple App Store ads | CPI in Qatar is QAR 25–80 — burns the whole budget for ~50 installs. ROI < creator partnership. |
| Multiple influencers | At $1k, one strong post > four weak posts. Add creator #2 in month 2. |
| PR agency | Reach out to Doha News, ILoveQatar, The Peninsula founder-direct in week 3. Free. |
| Branded merch | Zero retention impact at launch. Defer 3 months. |
| Translation (Arabic) | Phase 2. English-first per `gtm-qatar.md` §1. |
| Product Hunt push tooling | Free if launched right. Defer to month 2 launch moment. |
| Snapchat sponsored lens | $1.5k+ in Qatar. Geofilter at $50/wk gives 80% of the value. |

## Risk lines

- **Anchor micro-influencer declines / over-charges.** Backup: pivot $300 to two smaller creators ($120 each, mid-tier 20–40k Qatar followers) + $60 add'l IG boost.
- **No venues sign in week 1.** Don't pour week 2 budget into ads — extend venue acquisition another week. The two-sided marketplace breaks without supply.
- **Sign-ups outpace venues.** Cap waitlist visibly ("we're at 412 / 500 first sign-ups") to manufacture urgency rather than over-promise activity in the app.
- **Cashback economics break.** Cap at QAR 200 / user / month (already in `gtm-qatar.md` §6). Venue funds 50% of cashback per partner agreement.

## What this $1,000 will and won't do

**Will:**
- Get 8–12 venue partners signed and live
- Get one strong micro-influencer to deliver one piece of credibility content
- Drive 2,000–3,000 verified sign-ups
- Validate cashback unit economics with real redemptions
- Give you data to raise / spend the next $10k from a position of evidence

**Will not:**
- Make Zizo Circle a household name in Qatar (that's a 3–6 month story)
- Reach Qatari nationals at scale (Snap-first creator strategy needed in month 2)
- Cover Arabic localization (phase 2 per `gtm-qatar.md`)
- Survive a venue-side cashback margin failure — model unit economics carefully before week 3 paid push.
